# HR Analytics Dashboard | Tableau Project

## 1. Project Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Tableau project designed to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, and employee distribution across various departments and age groups.

This dashboard transforms raw HR data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations, helping organizations understand employee turnover trends and make data-driven HR decisions.

---

## 2. Project Objective

The main objectives of this project are:

* To analyze employee attrition and retention patterns.
* To monitor workforce demographics.
* To identify departments with high employee turnover.
* To evaluate employee satisfaction across job roles.
* To analyze attrition based on age group, gender, and education field.
* To create an interactive HR analytics dashboard for business decision-making.

## 7. Key Insights Generated

Important HR insights identified from the dashboard include:

* The overall employee attrition rate is 16.12%.
* Research & Development (R&D) department contributes the highest employee attrition.
* Employees aged 25–34 represent the largest workforce segment.
* Male employees show higher attrition compared to female employees.
* Life Sciences employees experience the highest attrition among education fields.
* Job satisfaction levels vary significantly across different job roles.
* Younger employees are more likely to leave the organization compared to senior employees.
